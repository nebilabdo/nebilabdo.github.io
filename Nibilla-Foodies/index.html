Our Special Menu

Shiro(ሺሮ)

A tasty stew made from chickpea flour, served with injera.

Doro Wat(ዶሮ ዋት)

A spicy chicken stew with hard-boiled eggs, served with injera.

Kitfo(ቅቶ)

Minced beef mixed with spiced butter and served with injera.

Burger(ቡርጌር)

A sandwich with a beef patty, lettuce, tomato, and pickles.

Pizza(ፒዛ)

A dough base topped with cheese, sauce, and various toppings.

Paella(ፓኤላ)

A rice dish with seafood, chicken, Tomatoes and vegetables.

Tibs(ቲብስ)

Sautéed meat with onions, garlic, and spices.

Sushi(ሱሺ)

Rice rolls with fresh fish, vegetables, and seaweed.
